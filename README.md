# Bugicide
### Developed for Ludum Dare 56

Play as a child who enjoys killing insects in his garden.
Your aim is to achieve the highest score using the skills available to you, depending on the time of day.

## Controls
* Move camera
  #### W/A/S/D
* Use skills
  #### 1/2/3

## Scoring
* Ant: 1 point
* Spider: 50 points
* Beetle: 500 points
